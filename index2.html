Part 1: Zinthara — The Last City

Earth—once full of oceans, forests, and thriving cities—had turned into a massive wasteland. Sandstorms danced across dead continents, and heatwaves cracked the ground like shattered glass. The world was a graveyard. But one city still lived.

"Zinthara—The Last City".

It wasn’t paradise. There were no forests. No blue rivers. No freedom.
Just enough water to survive. Just enough shelter to keep breathing.
Zinthara had life—but not peace.
Its system turned survival into a burden.
And yet, across the endless deserts, people still crawled to reach it. Because outside it, there was nothing.
Here, at least, there was a chance.
To breathe. To live.
