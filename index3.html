Part 1: Zinthara — The Last City

Above it floated a silent titan: Astralaya, the black, grey, and white metallic pyramid. It hovered like a god without mercy—never touching the ground, never resting. Some called it The Last Authority, though most feared to name it at all.

Astralaya drained the Earth’s final resources to feed itself. Zinthara survived—barely—beneath its shadow.
And within its metal chambers ruled a man feared across the scorched Earth.
Supreme Skyler, the Black Mantle.
He wore a long black overcoat from collar to sole, black boots, black pants—every inch of him screamed command.

He wasn’t a silent tyrant—he was loud.
Resistance wasn’t an option
It didn’t protect Zinthara.
It didn’t nourish it.
