Part 1: Zinthara — The Last City

It simply allowed the city to live—for its own reasons.
His robotic enforcers—cold, relentless—patrolled every corner, ensuring his will was absolute.
For the people of Zinthara, his word meant survival—and sometimes, death.

But even he didn’t stand alone.
There was someone—something—above him.
Hidden deep within Astralaya, beyond walls no man dared cross, was a chamber.
Lava poured down four of its walls. Water flowed from three.

Together they met and cooled into black obsidian at the center, forming a perfect ring.
Floating above it was the figure they whispered about—but never named aloud.

The X. Sometimes… The Unknown.
